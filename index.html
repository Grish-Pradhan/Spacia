Core Feature

Live Occupancy — See it before you go

Spacia pulls real-time crowd data from partner venue sensors and aggregated user check-ins. The occupancy meter refreshes every 3–5 minutes, so what you see is what you get when you arrive.

  • Colour-coded crowd indicators (green / yellow / red)
  • Average visit duration so you know wait times
  • Historical trend chart for each venue
  • Push alerts when your saved spot drops to "quiet"
📡
AI Engine

Smart Predictions powered by ML

Our in-house machine learning model analyses patterns from over 8 months of local venue data. It predicts busyness up to 3 hours ahead — letting you plan study blocks, meetings, and creative sessions around reality, not hope.

  • 3-hour occupancy forecast per venue
  • Trained specifically on Kathmandu, Pokhara & Lalitpur patterns
  • Adjusts for public holidays, load-shedding hours, and local events
  • Gets smarter with every check-in
🤖
Vibe & Filters

Vibe Intelligence — match mood to space

Every venue in our network is tagged by the community across 12 dimensions — noise level, natural light, WiFi reliability, power outlets, seating comfort, and more. Filter by what matters to you and surface only the spaces that fit.

  • Quiet / moderate / buzzy noise tagging
  • WiFi speed verified monthly (in Mbps)
  • Study-friendly vs. social vs. creative space labels
  • Nepali tea / coffee availability, of course ☕
🎯
Community

Reviews & Community Tips

Spacia is built on trust. Every rating comes from a verified visit. Users share short tips like "best corner table for calls" or "avoid after 4pm on Fridays" — the kind of local knowledge no map app can give you.

  • Verified-visit reviews only
  • Quick tip cards from regulars
  • Upvote helpful tips
  • Report outdated info instantly

The Problem We're Solving

Every day, thousands of students, freelancers, and remote workers in Kathmandu waste 20–40 minutes travelling to cafés, libraries, or co-working spaces — only to find them packed, noisy, or lacking power sockets.

Map apps show you ratings from three months ago. Review platforms tell you what a space is like on average — not right now. Nobody provides real-time intelligence about crowd levels, noise, or vibe.

Spacia changes this. We combine user check-ins and machine learning to create Nepal's first real-time, intent-matched space discovery platform. The result? No more wasted trips. No more settling. Just the right space, right when you need it.

Frequently Asked

How accurate is the occupancy data?

+
We aggregate multiple signals — user check-ins, time-of-day patterns, and historical data — to estimate occupancy. In venues with active check-ins the data is very reliable. In quieter venues our predictions are based on historical models, which may be less precise.

Does Spacia work without internet?

+
Pro and Teams subscribers can cache their saved venues for offline access. Live occupancy and search require an internet connection.

My favourite café isn't on Spacia. How do I add it?

+
Tap the "Suggest a venue" button in the app (Settings → Suggest a Venue) and submit the details. We review suggestions and onboard qualifying venues within 2–3 weeks.

Can I delete my account?

+
Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. All your data is permanently removed within 30 days, in line with our privacy policy.

Which payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept eSewa, Khalti, ConnectIPS, and major credit/debit cards. All payments are processed in Nepali Rupees (NPR).
